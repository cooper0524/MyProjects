Conversation Starter

Choose a news topic, and just swipe left or right to see the headlines, if you don't have time to read the headline?  just press the speaker button at the bottom of the box, and it will be read to you.  Have a new conversation with every passenger, get to know current events, and after the trip, if you want to read on, just tap the headline, and go to the news article to read more.

Disclaimer: Please only choose a headline, or read a news article when you are stationary.
